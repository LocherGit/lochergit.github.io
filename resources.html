Chiptunes Jukebox

Here is a jukebox that plays a random tune from a collection of tracks I covered. Making these took a long time so I am quite thankful to have a plattform to show them off. They've been optimized to be playable on a real gameboy.

Just press on [Randomize] or [Show Music Catalogue] to get started.
